Propositions


We ask all
CORPORATIONS to...

... reduce production of unnecessary plastics in packaging, and to commit to incorporating recycled plastic or other sustainable alternatives for their products. We also ask corporations to dispose of plastic waste in a responsible manner that prevents waste from ending up in the ocean.

We ask all
GOVERNMENTS to...

... establish national targets for sustainability measures to reduce pollutant output, and to incentivize industries to meet these targets. We also ask governments to work at subnational levels to ensure that our communities are adhering to new sustainability standards.

We ask YOU to...

... engage with your government representatives to ensure that they keep sustainability in mind throughout their term, and to ask them to manage waste in a transparent & accountable manner. We also ask individuals to use their power as consumers to rely less on single-use plastics and consider investing in eco-friendly alternatives.
